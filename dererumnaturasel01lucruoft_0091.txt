LUCRETIUS v. 780—2

780. nune redeo. Lucretius will now return after the long

digression beginning 1. 509.
mollia . . ., ‘ the fields of the earth in their tender age.’

781. luminis oras: see note, l. 85.

781-2. quid . . . crerint τ indirect question after some verb such
as resolvo understood from redeo. crerint contracted for creverint
=decreverint3 cf. Catull. lxiv. 150 germanum amittere crevi. In
1. 393 cernere = decernere, but in a slightly different sense.

committere ventis: Verg. Aen. xi. 560 quae nunc dubiis
committitur auris.
